    Partner World Wide Technology

    World Wide Technology (WWT), a global technology solutions provider that combines strategy and execution to deliver transformational outcomes, and MobiledgeX help organizations accelerate global edge computing deployments at scale by delivering edge cloud solutions to more quickly drive ROI of infrastructure investments.

    The partnership lets customers leverage certified infrastructure that is immediately ready to monetize by fulfilling next-gen application and device requirements for developers worldwide.

    WWT’s Converged Edge Platform (CEP) is a pre-built infrastructure solution with the MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud platform that helps operators leverage choice, trust and control over aggregated edge compute and processing resources — both public and private — in conjunction with 4G and 5G infrastructure to rapidly rollout nationwide edge networks that can support innovative new mobile services.

    The CEP is available in WWT’s Advanced Technology Center (ATC), a state-of-the-art multi-vendor lab environment that acts as an innovation enabler to ensure the best possible commercial addressability when placed in mobile edge networks.  

    Check out the Converged Edge Platform (CEP) lab: HERE

    As MobiledgeX’s sole distributor, WWT delivers fully integrated edge infrastructure on which MobiledgeX software runs and the validated customer premise equipment required to consume edge services via its four global integration centers.

    Given WWT’s role in the 5G delivery ecosystem, edge solutions need to be vertically focused and strategically deployed at scale. By more quickly integrating and deploying edge computing globally, service providers can focus on identifying vertical use cases and related applications their end customers will ultimately consume.
