    Partner VMware

    Mobile network operators, MNOs, are embracing digital transformation. They’re looking to a cloud-native approach to 5G to help them compete and thrive in the digital era. That’s where the 5G-ready VMware Telco Cloud Platform together with the MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud platform can help MNOs. Leveraging these market-proven solutions enables you to:

    • Introduce elastic business models

    • Streamline operations

    • Minimize network infrastructure costs, and 

    • Introduce new revenue-producing services for both telecommunications and enterprise workloads.

    Since 2018, MobiledgeX has been working with MNOs to deploy an edge cloud platform that extends deep into MNO networks and as close as possible to end-users. At the same time, VMware has partnered with over 100 service providers from around the world to deploy telco-grade virtualization and automation solutions which accelerate service innovation and simplify network operations.

    Together, the integrated solution brings cloud to the edge. Integrated with cellular infrastructure brings intelligence to the edge so MNOs and their customers  can create new and improved user experiences. Leading ISVs and channel partners provide a portfolio of  vertical-specific applications to provide more options and facilitate deployments.

    VMware and MobiledgeX simplify edge complexity, secure edge infrastructure at scale, and accelerate innovation with an ubiquitous, digital Edge-to-Cloud foundation.
