    Partner Google

    MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud platform integration with Google Anthos brings the power and resources of the public cloud to mobile network operators and the enterprise for maximum infrastructure choice and workload control.

    With Google Anthos, mobile network operators have the freedom to choose how they want to manage their multi-vendor telco edge cloud infrastructure and applications while retaining full control of data, security, and privacy.

    MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud platform provides a universal control plane to unify management over existing virtualized infrastructure into a single pane view to provide real time application and infrastructure monitoring, notification, and metrics.  Customers can automate workload orchestration using auto-scale and auto-provisioning features to leverage public cloud infrastructure and services. 

    To experience or learn more about MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud with Google Anthos, request an executive briefing or technical demonstration at partners@mobiledgex.com
