    Join Vasanth Mohan, Developer Advocate at MobiledgeX, and AR/VR innovators (e.g. NVIDIA, T-Mobile, Mawari, Sturfee) for “5G EDGE COMPUTE : ESSENTIAL INFRASTRUCTURE TO SCALE AR & VR”, an AWE USA 2021 hosted panel discussion on Nov 11th 9:30am-10:25am PT.

     The focus of the panel will be the criticality of GPU compute offloading to the edge remote edge cloud delivered services (e.g. Rendering, Visual Positioning Systems, Computer Vision) in a mobile environment with resource constrained AR/VR devices. The panel will  include the following topics:

    •    Intro to 5G and Edge by T-Mobile

    •    Video case study demonstrating how MobiledgeX and panel partners work together to deliver compelling AR/VR experiences

    •    How each panelist’s company fits within the AR/VR services stack

    •    Each company’s vision on how to move from PoC to Scalability

    •    Enterprise / Industrial AR/VR use cases 5 minutes

    •    Q&A
