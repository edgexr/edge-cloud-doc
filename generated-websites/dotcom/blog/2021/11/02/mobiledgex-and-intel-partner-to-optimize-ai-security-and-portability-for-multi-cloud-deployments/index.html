    VP, Partnerships & Alliances

    MobiledgeX and Intel have partnered to integrate Intel’s 3rd Gen Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors for built-in AI, crypto acceleration and advanced security capabilities for mobile edge infrastructure, services and applications. This integration is now available on MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud, a universal multi-cloud orchestration and automation solution for mobile network operators and enterprises to manage edge infrastructure, workloads, and security operations. The partnership aims to explore many other Intel technologies of critical relevance at the edge, using including Intel® Smart Edge Open edge computing toolkit and the Intel™ distribution of the OpenVINO™ toolkit.

    Our partnership helps mobile network operators improve hardware performance, lower TCO and power flexible portability for highly optimized multi-cloud environments across edge, network, and cloud for scalable use case deployments like AI, video analytics, and cloud-native network functions. The integration of 3rd Gen Intel™ Xeon™ Scalable processors gives mobile network operators:

    • Flexible performance: Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors deliver industry leading, workload optimized performance, with built-in AI acceleration, providing a seamless performance foundation, to help speed data’s transformative impact, from edge to cloud.

    • Advanced Security Technologies: Intel brings enhanced data protection by design to the mobile operator and enterprise. Intel’s hardware-enhanced security helps to thwart malicious exploits while maintaining workload integrity and performance. Encryption efficiency to provide trusted data service delivery at rest, in use, and in-flight.

    • Customization and Scale: Intel accelerates platform innovation and hardware-enhanced virtualization across compute, network, storage, all with support for a new class of memory innovation that fuels the cost-efficient, flexible, scalable edge-to-cloud, to consistently deliver business-to-business and business-to-consumer experiences.

    MobiledgeX and Intel’s latest collaboration builds on joint work contributed by MobiledgeX and Intel to the GSMA Operator Platform Telco Edge Requirements as participants of the GSMA Operator Platform Group.

    See also our newly released Intel Network Builders Solution Brief on Building On-Demand Edge Clouds from Mixed Infrastructure, including new use cases on volumetric streaming and device offloading for foot traffic analysis. 

    Our partner enablement builds on joint work contributed by MobiledgeX and Intel to the GSMA Operator Platform Telco Edge Requirements as participants of the GSMA Operator Platform Group. 

    To experience or learn more about MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud integration with Intel, reach out to us at partners@mobiledgex.com to request an executive briefing and technical demonstration.
