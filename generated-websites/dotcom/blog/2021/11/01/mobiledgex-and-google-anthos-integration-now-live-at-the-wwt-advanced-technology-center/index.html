    MobiledgeX and Google Anthos Integration Now Live at the WWT Advanced Technology Center

    VP, Partnerships & Alliances

    VP, Partnerships & Alliances

    MobiledgeX on Anthos delivers maximum infrastructure choice and workload control for the mobile network operator and enterprise customer.

    MobiledgeX’s integration with Google Anthos is now available to trial in World Wide Technology’s Advanced Technology Center (ATC), a one-of-a-kind virtual lab environment to bypass the chaotic technology landscape and quickly come to smart conclusions about innovative solutions.

    MobiledgeX and WWT partnership brings new choice and opportunity for mobile network operators to leverage public cloud infrastructure and services as they modernize their edge infrastructure, deploy workloads across any cloud, launch new edge services and validate edge native applications on their network environment.

    The addition of Google Anthos in the ATC represents an expansion of WWT’s Telco Edge Blueprint. Customers now have the choice to deploy on private infrastructure and use the MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud 3.0 platform to manage workloads across any cloud environment, from telco edge cloud to public cloud and on-prem.

    By expanding WWT Telco Edge Blueprint, MobiledgeX can bring new choices like Google Anthos to help our customers increase performance and lower operational costs at scale. WWT has long provided our customers with tools and services to help them de-risk their infrastructure investments. With MobiledgeX and Google Anthos, we can offer new software infrastructure models to help customers validate their edge strategies, enable go to market for their edge services and enable cost savings at scale.

    — Kraig Ecker, SVP, Global Service Provider Sales, WWT

    Mobile network operators and enterprise customers can explore and validate their edge strategies at the WWT ATC today. A sample of resources available are:

    • Telco Blueprint expansion with MobiledgeX on Google Anthos for any cloud deployment

    • Test and validation environment for multi-operator 4G/5G

    • Edge infrastructure configuration diversity including Tier 1 blueprints for US/Europe/Asia, Standalone 5G and Private Edge with CBRS 

    • Workload transit validation across any edge infrastructure or cloud

    • Testing and validation of emerging telco edge services and edge native applications 

    • Interoperability services and facilities for telco guidance, specifications, and standards

    MobiledgeX and WWT’s ongoing partnership brings new choice and opportunity for mobile network operators to leverage public cloud infrastructure and services as they modernize edge infrastructure, deploy workloads across any cloud, launch new edge services and validate edge native applications. 

    WWT ATC is purpose-built for mobile network operators and enterprise customers to design, test and validate infrastructure solutions, global industry standards and enable new services and applications for the global marketplace. The center is also home to the WWT Edge Innovation Studio, a proving ground for edge native applications and services that deliver incredible end user experiences and drive new revenue streams for organizations that provide them.Customers, developers, and ISVs can test and validate their products for telco edge cloud, including deployment to MobiledgeX production cloud by joining the MobiledgeX Early Access Program. 

    To experience or learn more about MobiledgeX Edge-Cloud integration with Google Anthos or how to experience the WWT Advanced Technology Center, request a brief or technical demonstration at partners@mobiledgex.com.
