    Privacy Policy

    Last modified: November 19, 2021

    1. Introduction

    MobiledgeX, Inc., ("Company", “Us”, “Our” or "We") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

    This policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes the types of information We may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the website www.MobiledgeX.com (our "Website") and Our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting and disclosing that information. Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Privacy Policy have the meanings ascribed to them in the Website’s Terms of Use.

    This Privacy Policy applies to information We collect:

    • On the Website.
    • In e-mail, text and other electronic messages between you and Us.
    • Through mobile and desktop applications, which provide dedicated non-browser-based interaction between you and this Website and Our Services.

    It does not apply to information collected by:

    • Us offline or through any other means, including information processed through the use of Services operated by Us, on behalf of a third party who independently collects your information; or
    • any third party, including through any application or content (including advertising) that may link to or be accessible from or on the Website.

    Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand Our policies and practices regarding your information and how We will treat it. If you choose not to “opt-in” to our Privacy Policy, or to not accept cookies, Flash cookies, or web beacons,  your use of the Website or Services could be limited, and use of Services may be unavailable. By consenting to the use of cookies, Flash cookies, and or web beacons on this Website and/or Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy may change from time to time.  Your continued use of this Website and Services after We make changes may include needing to accept a revised consent to the handling of your information is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the Privacy Policy periodically for updates.

    2. Children Under the Age of 13

    Our Website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. No one under age 13 may provide any information to or on the Website or on or through any Services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, do not use or provide any information on this Website or on or through any Services or any of the Website features, make any purchases through the Website, use any Services or provide any information about yourself to Us, including your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address or any screen name or user name you may use. If We learn We have collected or received personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, We will delete that information. If you believe We might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at legal@MobiledgeX.com.

    3. Information We Collect About You and How We Collect It

    We collect several types of information from and about users of Our Website and Services, including information:

    • by which you may be personally identified, such as name, company affiliation, postal address, e-mail address, and telephone number ("personal information"); and/or
    • about your internet connection, the equipment you use to access the Website and Services including identity, location and usage details.

    We collect this information:

    • Directly from you when you provide it to Us.
    • Automatically as you navigate through the Website. Information collected automatically through cookies and other tracking technologies may also include usage details, and IP addresses.

    3.1 Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies

    As you navigate through and interact with the Website and Services, We may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions and patterns, including:

    • Details of your visits to the Website, including traffic data, location data, logs and other communication data and the resources that you access and use on the Website.
    • Information about your computer and internet connection, including your IP address, operating system and browser type.

    The information We collect automatically is statistical data and does not include personal information, but We may maintain it or associate it with personal information We collect in other ways or receive from third parties. It helps us to improve the Website and Services  and to deliver better and a more personalized service, including by enabling us to:

    • Estimate Our audience size and usage patterns.
    • Store information about your preferences, allowing Us to customize the Website and Services according to your individual interests.
    • Speed up your searches.
    • Recognize you when you return to the Website or applications.

    The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:

    • Cookies (or browser cookies). A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of the Website.
    • Web Beacons. Pages of the Website and our e-mails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags and single-pixel gifs) that permit the Company, for example, to count users who have visited those pages or opened an e-mail and for other related website statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain website content and verifying system and server integrity).

    We will use the above technologies only if we have received your consent through the “opt-in” window on Our homepage.

    3.2 Third-party Use of Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies.

    Google Analytics

    The Website uses Google Analytics to collect information about use of this site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit this site, what pages they visit when they do so, and what other sites they used prior to coming to this site. We use the information we get from Google Analytics only to improve this site. Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit this site, rather than your name or other identifying information. We do not combine the information collected through the use of Google Analytics with personally identifiable information. Although Google Analytics plants a permanent cookie on your web browser to identify you as a unique user the next time you visit this site, the cookie cannot be used by anyone but Google. Google’s ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your visits to this site is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy.

    Sendinblue

    The Website uses Sendinblue, a third party marketing service that provides analytics related to the Website. The information Sendinblue directly collects via tracking is not personal information. However, it may be combined with other data We collect, which, together, may represent personal information. Information such as IP addresses and engagement with the Website may be collected through Sendinblue's use of cookies, web beacons, embedded scripts, or any other similar mechanism that may be used to monitor the Website traffic. Sendinblue allows Us to analyze the visitors to the Website, and how We may most appropriately deliver more information to them about the Services. The processes and mechanisms Sendinblue uses to provide their analytics and services are solely governed by their respective privacy policy, and not MobiledgeX’s Privacy Policy.

    The Website also uses Sendinblue to collect the data you submit to us through filling in forms on the Website, which is combined with the tracking information.

    For more information about Sendinblue, including their own privacy policy, and how they handle and use your data, please visit https://www.sendinblue.com/legal/privacypolicy/.

    CookieYes

    The Website also uses CookieYes to allow users to choose which cookies they will accept. This tool collects IP addresses of users who accept or reject cookies. The data collected by CookieYes is governed by their respective privacy policy, not MobiledgeX's. This policy is available at https://www.cookieyes.com/privacy-policy/.

    7. Choices About How We Use and Disclose Your Information

    We strive to provide you with choices regarding the personal information you provide to Us. We have created mechanisms to provide you with the following control over your information:

    • Tracking Technologies and Advertising. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this site may then be inaccessible or not function properly.
