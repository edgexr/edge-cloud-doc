    Leadership Team

    The MobiledgeX leadership team has unparalleled experience in business, science and technology.

    Jason Hoffman

    President and Chief Executive Officer

    Jason Hoffman is President and CEO of MobiledgeX, where his decades of experience in telecommunications and cloud infrastructure have driven him to design the future of mobile edge services, applications, and devices.

    Jason is considered one of the architects of large scale cloud computing, having led the development of container technologies, asynchronous, high concurrency runtimes, and converged server, storage, and networking systems. For over a decade, he was CTO and Founder of pioneering IaaS and software provider Joyent (now owned by Samsung). Here, Jason ran product, engineering, operations, and commercial management for a decade, launching the most popular asynchronous runtime (node.js), most secure KVM-based VMs, as well as the world’s first serverless and first container-as-a-service offering.

    Most recently, Jason was CTO for Digital Services at Ericsson, where he led a 30,000 person unit responsible for the company’s multinational cloud and datacenter infrastructure business. While at Ericsson, his group led the market in the modernization of telecom infrastructure and created the world’s first hyper-scale, disaggregated system.

    Leah Maher

    General Counsel & Chief Operating Officer

    Leah Maher is General Counsel and COO of MobiledgeX. Her experience includes 25+ years of providing legal advice and business development initiatives in both public and private companies.

    Before joining MobiledgeX, she served as head of operations product area cloud systems at Ericsson AB a mobile network infrastructure and software company.

    Prior to Ericsson, she was the general counsel for Joyent, Inc. a public cloud infrastructure and enterprise software company. Her prior experience includes legal and business development roles at Force10 Networks, SpectraLink, American Tower, PictureTel, Parametric Technology and Prime Computer.

    Leah holds a B.A. degree in economics and government from Colby College and a J.D. degree from the University of Akron School of Law.

    Ulf Andersson

    Chief Revenue Officer

    Ulf has spent his entire 25+ year career working in the telecom industry in various leadership roles ranging from R&D, Product Line management, Partnering and Alliances to Business Development and Sales.

    Before joining MobiledgeX, Ulf worked at Ericsson heading up sales teams for various Operators in North America, the latest being AT&T.

    Ulf has Bachelors degrees in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering from Helsinki Technical Institute, and an MBA from University of Dallas.

    Michael Lochead

    SVP Product

    Michael Lochead is SVP of Product at MobiledgeX. Michael brings over two decades of leadership in cloud computing, DevOps, open source and infrastructure software.

    Prior to MobiledgeX, Michael built and led global product and services teams at high growth companies in Silicon Valley including cloud pioneer Joyent, and was a co-founder at Scalr, a Gartner MQ leader for cloud management platforms.

    Michael spent over 11 years at Oracle Corporation and served on the senior leadership team for Oracle's Support and Services Organization.

    Michael holds a B.A from St. Lawrence University.
