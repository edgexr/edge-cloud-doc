    Dutch Guild Multi-Media Meeting: 5G and Media

    April 2, 2019 8:00 pm to 11:30 pm

    Location: Hague, Netherlands

    The Dutch Guild of Multi-Media Engineers is an informal club of enthusiastic technicians from the media world, who exchange knowledge and put topics on the agenda via lecture evenings. On April 2, experts will explain what 5G will mean for media, both in production and in distribution.
