    Edge Application Developer Hackathon

    April 30, 2019 11:00 am to 6:00 pm

    Location: Menlo Park, CA

    The edge is no longer a concept, it is a reality. Companies like Niantic, the creators of Pokemon GO, are leveraging the power of edge computing to bring new experiences to life and changing user expectations for application performance.

    Join us for this exclusive event to see how your application can benefit from edge computing. Experts from leading edge computing companies like MobiledgeX, Facebook, and Intel will guide participants through the available infrastructure and are sponsoring prizes for winning teams.

    Each team will be provided a Samsung Galaxy S10 handset for development during the event to conduct their hack for the judges.
